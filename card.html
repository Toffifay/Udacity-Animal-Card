Moon Jellyfish

The 4 white circles are the reproductive organs that can be seen on top of the Moon Jellyfish

  • Scientific Name: Aurelia aurita
  • Average Length: 7.1"-16.5"
  • Average Lifespan: 8-12 months
  • Habitat: Surface water over reefs and along the coast

Some Moon Jellyfish are capable of Bioluminescence which means that they can glow in the dark

They can survive in any temperature of water, but they prefer to stay close to the surface after hatching from the ocean floor to feed on plankton sized animals

Even though Jellyfish are known to just float by the current's whim, Moon Jellyfish actually have coordinated movement that is guided by a primary sensory like you and I that respond to outside stimulus like Light and Touch
